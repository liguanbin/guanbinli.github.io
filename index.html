Biography

I am currently a research associate professor in School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University. I received my PhD degree in computer science from the University of Hong Kong. My supervisor is Prof. Yizhou Yu. Before that, I received the B. S. degree in Computer Science and Technology and M. S. degree in Computer Software and Theory from Sun Yat-sen University in 2009 and 2012 respectively.
